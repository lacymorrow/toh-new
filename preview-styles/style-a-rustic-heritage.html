February 12, 2026

Spring Road Maintenance Schedule Announced

The Town Council has approved the 2026 spring road maintenance schedule. Work will begin on Harmony Highway in March and continue through May.

February 8, 2026

Community Garden Plot Registration Now Open

Residents can now register for community garden plots at the Harmony Community Center. Plots are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

February 1, 2026

Town Hall Renovation Project Update

The restoration of the historic Town Hall building is on schedule. The council expects to reopen the building for public use by late spring.
